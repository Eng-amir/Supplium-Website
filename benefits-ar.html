Benefits

how can supplium help you

Order Automation

When your stock drops below a certain level, Supplium automatically sends a purchase order to the suppliers hat you predefined.

Analytics & Statistics

Supplium dashboard is the on-demand control center that gives you an overview of all your purchases on a periodical basis. With a click of a button, you can generate reports that will help you be better informed about your purchases so you can negotiate better deals with your suppliers.

Automated SOA (Statement of Accounts)

If you are using credit with your suppliers, Supplium automatically generates a Statement of Account (SOA) for each supplier so you can double check your accounts and invoices at the end of each credit period.

Credit Tracker

Don’t let the credit period statement date creep up on you again. Supplium shows you a countdown for each of your suppliers’ credit limit, credit periods & payment due dates.

Personalized Suppliers and Products Catalogs

Your personalized catalog helps you manage & organize your suppliers & products list so you can replenish your stock with great ease.

Exclusive Suppliers Database

Thinking of a new recipe? Search our database of more than 1700 suppliers for any new products that you wish to purchase & see which suppliers sell them. You can view and compare the prices of different suppliers and communicate with them easily. Once you have made a deal with a supplier, you can add them to your personalized suppliers' catalog and start ordering!

Consolidated Orders

If you have many branches, Supplium gives you the freedom to consolidate all your purchase orders to be delivered to either to your centralized warehouse or to your different branches.

Free Training

Supplium offers free training to your employees to prepare them to use the system. Certificate of completion will be provided at the end of the training period.

Multiple User Roles and Users permissions

You have the ultimate control over your employees' roles and permissions for using Supplium. Give access to your chef, for example, to submit orders, while your accountant can only access the analytics and statistics.

Unlimited Number of Users

You can give access to as many employees as you like to use Supplium.
